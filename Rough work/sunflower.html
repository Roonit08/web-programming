Sunflower

Sunflowers are known for their bright yellow petals and tall stems. They follow the movement of the sun across the sky, a behavior called heliotropism, symbolizing warmth and positivity. These cheerful flowers are often associated with happiness, energy, and optimism. Sunflowers grow best in sunny locations and can reach impressive heights, making them a striking addition to gardens and fields. Their large, round faces and vibrant colors make them a favorite in art and decoration. Overall, sunflowers embody the bright and uplifting spirit of summer and sunshine.
