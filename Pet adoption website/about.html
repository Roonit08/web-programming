About Happy Paws

We’re a small rescue group focused on helping homeless cats and dogs find stable, caring homes. Our goal is simple: make adoption feel welcoming, clear, and safe for both people and pets.

What we do

Happy Paws works with foster carers and local supporters to rescue animals who need a second chance. We help with basic health checks, socialization, and matching each pet with an adopter that fits.

Why adoption matters

Adoption helps reduce the number of animals living without shelter. It also supports responsible pet ownership, because adoption is more than “taking a pet home” it’s a long-term commitment.

Our approach

  • Be honest about a pet’s needs (energy level, training, temperament).
  • Keep the adoption process simple and beginner-friendly.
  • Encourage safe, respectful handling and long-term care.
