Adoption Information

Adopting is exciting, but it’s also a real responsibility. Here’s the basic process we use so it stays fair, safe, and simple.

Step-by-step process

  1. Browse the Available Pets page and pick a few you connect with.
  2. Send an enquiry through the Contact page (tell us your lifestyle and experience).
  3. Meet & greet (if possible) to make sure it’s a good match.
  4. Home prep (basic supplies, safe spaces, food, bedding, and a routine).
  5. Adoption day — we confirm details and share care tips to help the pet settle in.

Common conditions

  • Adopters must be able to provide safe housing and ongoing care.
  • Some pets may be better in quiet homes or with experienced owners.
  • If renting, you should confirm pets are allowed before applying.

Before you adopt

  • Plan a budget (food, vet, grooming, emergency costs).
  • Think about time: walks, training, play, and attention.
  • Choose a pet based on lifestyle, not just looks.
  • Give the first few weeks extra patience change can be stressful for animals.
